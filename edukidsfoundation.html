About Edu-Kids Foundation

At Edu-Kids Foundation, we firmly believe that education is a fundamental right for every child, essential for their holistic development and future success. Unfortunately, numerous children in Africa, particularly in Nigeria, encounter significant barriers to accessing quality education, leading to enduring negative consequences in their lives. As a non-profit organization, we are committed to providing educational and social welfare support to OVC.

Our mission revolves around tackling the educational challenges faced by OVC in Nigeria and other developing countries through the implementation of various support programs. Currently, our core initiatives include the student support project, OVC feeding program, scholarship program, sanitary hygiene program, and counseling & monitoring program. At Edu-Kids Foundation, we firmly believe that every child, regardless of their background or financial circumstances, deserves equal opportunities to pursue education and realize their dreams.

How you can support:

  • Student Support Project: Includes provision of school uniforms, bags, socks, textbooks, and mathematical sets.
  • OVC Feeding Program: Alleviate hunger and malnutrition by providing a year school lunch pack.
  • Scholarship Program: Provides fund that cover standardized tests to be admitted into the University/College (WAEC/NECO).
  • Sanitary Hygiene: Provides one-year sanitary pad for female students.
